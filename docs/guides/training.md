## Drop-ins

The quickest way to get help is to come to one of our drop-in sessions — **no appointment needed**.

### Biosciences

#### Data Science

Come along to the **Darwin Common Room** for a drop-in with Biosciences Data Scientist **James Gilbert**. We can discuss any questions you have about your data, code, or analyses.

#### Bioimage Analysis

Drop-ins with the bioimage analysis team: [uclccmd.co.uk/drop-in](https://www.uclccmd.co.uk/drop-in)

#### General Computational

ARC also offers regular drop-in sessions with support on a range of topics, including coding, data management and using HPCs. More info: [ucl.ac.uk/advanced-research-computing/community/drop-ins-and-surgeries](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/advanced-research-computing/community/drop-ins-and-surgeries)

**Overview:** Work in this area involves developing and applying algorithms for biological data analysis, including statistical learning, deep learning, and data engineering. Core skills include Python programming, statistics, and understanding of ML pipelines.

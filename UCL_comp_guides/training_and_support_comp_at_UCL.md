### Training Profiles
Want to improve your comp skills but not sure where to start? First, we recommend some of the general trainings linked above. Here, we provide more info about skillsets required in different computational biology roles, based on the research themes listed on the (Comp Bio webpages)[https://www.ucl.ac.uk/life-sciences/biosciences/computational-biology]. We give an overview of the general and specific skills required, as well as some relevant examples of courses available to undergrads and postgrads.
